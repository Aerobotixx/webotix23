Why Us

We Are Makers - We strongly believe in practicals

We strongly believe in practical learning than being book-worms. Industry believes in your practical skills than marks & grades. Hence we provide training only in practical & hands-on manner which strongly promote DIY attitude in students. This helps these future engineers to think wisely and find solutions practically while working in industry.

  • 01 No Boring Lectures - Only Hands-on Sessions

    We conduct our training sessions are based on hands-on approach. Participants perform practical experiments on our self made kits. It helps them to learn technologies without buying expensive stuff and kits without having any proper guidance. After our training, participants can even buy our customized kits.

  • 02 No CTRL + C & CTRL + V Policy

    Copy Pasting is the most dangerous threat in learning. It just spreads half-knowledge which is extremely dangerous than having no knowledge. We have no Ctrl + C & Ctrl + V policy for our most of the trainings. Every participant has to perform on his own as per taught in the training.

  • 03 Say No to Readymade Kits : Only DIY

    There is no such a place for readymade things when you learn. All you have to is DIY - Do-It-Yourself. We dont provide any readymade kits to participants. We ask them to make experiments from very basics by making connections and installations from scratch. We provide all the stuff on use & return basis, to keep trainings cost effective for participants.

Our Services

Workshops, Trainings & Guest Lectures

We provide various short term & long term trainings for career development in Industry 4.0. All our trainings are carried out by our experts with timely updated syllabus and training resources. We have maintained the constant rising graph of successful workshops & trainings execution since our establishment in 2016. Arduino & IoT training is our most popular & demanded workshop in last few years after the aeromodelling training. We provide both offline as well as online (which ever is possible) trainings. We even deliver guest lectures in colleges & institutes to let students & faculties know about the latest as well as future trends of technologies.

UAV Development & Training

We are proud to be the first commercial UAV service provider in Kolhapur. We have a benchmark in UAV Development & Training. With our several years experience & strong industry collaborations we are providing UAV & target development as well as testing services to various government & private organizations in defence sector. We always try to provide high end & reliable solutions in UAV & Drone development. For engineering enthusiasts & passionate hobbyists, we even provide UAV / UAS development & flying training with certificate. We have our own aeromodelling community to explore the world of Aviation & Model Aeronautics.

Internships & Final Year Project Solutions

Along with latest technology stack trainings, we also provide Internships to students which helps them to get hand on experience of industrial development & operations. We only select candidates for internship by systematic process starting from interviews. Selected candidate gets chance to work on site with our team on live industry projects. This developes the profile as well as cultivates the new skill set of candidate to survive in this competitive world. We also help engineering students in development of their last year projects. We dont provide readymade projects, rather we develop projects from scratch with 100 % involvement of students entire development.

Software & Industrial Project Solutions

With our collaborations, we also develop software as well as hardware solutions for various industries. We are dealing in latest technology stacks of software as well as latest hardware development platforms like SBCs & Evaluation boards. We are providing high end solutions like ERP softwares, SPM monitoring software, android apps, IoT based control & monitoring applications, Energy Montoring systems etc. to automation as well as automotive industries. We also deal in development of customized CNC routers, special purpose machines, material handling systems, conveyor systems etc. We are providing most of the industrial softwares based on cloud, to simplify the operations like remote monitoring & analysis. Our vision is to let maximum industrial establishments get powered with Industry 4.0 features & advancements.
